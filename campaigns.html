Dental Awareness Campaigns

We actively lead and participate in dental awareness campaigns throughout the year to educate communities and promote preventive oral healthcare.

Our Campaigns

Spreading Dental Awareness

Each campaign is designed to educate, motivate, and provide accessible dental care to communities across India.

20 March · Annual

World Oral Health Day

Every year on 20th March, we celebrate World Oral Health Day by offering free dental check-ups, organising community awareness drives, and conducting social media campaigns to educate the public on the importance of oral hygiene. Our doctors deliver talks at schools, corporate offices, and community centres across Delhi NCR, reaching over 2,000 people each year.

Awareness Free Check-ups
February · Annual

National Dental Hygiene Month

Throughout February, our National Dental Month campaign focuses on preventive care. We run special offers on dental cleanings and check-ups, publish daily oral health tips on social media, and distribute free dental kits at partnering schools and offices. The campaign has reached over 10,000 individuals since inception and promotes regular dental visits as a cornerstone of preventive health.

National Prevention
Year-Round Initiative

Smile India Campaign

Our flagship Smile India Campaign is a year-round initiative dedicated to providing subsidised smile makeovers to individuals who cannot afford cosmetic dental care. From domestic abuse survivors to acid attack victims, we partner with NGOs to identify deserving candidates and provide transformative dental treatments at significantly reduced costs — giving them a reason to smile again.

Social Impact Smile Makeover
September · Annual

Sugar-Free September

Sugar is the number one cause of dental cavities. Our Sugar-Free September campaign challenges participants to reduce sugar intake for 30 days while educating them about the link between diet and oral health. We provide meal planning tips, sugar-alternative guides, and free cavity risk assessments at our clinic. Schools participate with poster competitions and pledge drives.

How You Can Participate

Our campaigns thrive because of community involvement. Whether you're a school administrator, corporate HR professional, NGO representative, or an individual passionate about dental health — there's a way for you to contribute.

We welcome partnerships for hosting dental awareness sessions, sponsoring dental kits for underprivileged children, volunteering at our free camps, and sharing our campaigns on social media to amplify their reach.
