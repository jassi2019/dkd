Cosmetic Tooth-Colored Fillings

Invisible composite restorations that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth — the modern, mercury-free alternative to silver amalgam fillings.

About This Treatment

Fillings That Disappear

Cosmetic tooth-colored fillings use advanced composite resin materials that are precisely shade-matched to your natural tooth colour. Unlike traditional silver amalgam fillings that are visually conspicuous, composite fillings are virtually invisible once placed, restoring both the function and aesthetics of your tooth.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we use premium nanohybrid composite resins that offer exceptional strength, polishability, and colour stability. These mercury-free materials bond directly to the tooth structure, allowing for more conservative preparations that preserve more of your healthy tooth. Whether you need a new filling or want to replace old silver fillings, our cosmetic composites deliver beautiful, long-lasting results.

  • Perfectly shade-matched to natural tooth colour
  • Mercury-free and biocompatible materials
  • Bonds to tooth for conservative preparation
  • Completed in a single 30–60 minute visit
  • Can replace old silver amalgam fillings
How It Works

The Filling Process

1

Decay Removal

Under local anaesthesia, the decayed portion of the tooth is carefully removed. For amalgam replacements, the old silver filling is safely extracted.

2

Shade Matching

The composite shade is precisely matched to your natural tooth colour using a shade guide, ensuring the filling blends invisibly once placed.

3

Layered Placement

Composite resin is applied in thin layers and each layer is cured with a special blue light. This builds up the restoration with natural-looking depth and translucency.

4

Shaping & Polishing

The filling is sculpted to match your tooth anatomy, then polished to a smooth, glossy finish. You leave with a restoration that is virtually undetectable.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do composite fillings last?

Modern composite fillings typically last 7–10 years with proper care, though many last significantly longer. Their longevity depends on the size and location of the filling, your bite forces, and oral hygiene habits. Front teeth fillings tend to last longer than those on heavily loaded back teeth.

Should I replace my silver fillings?

We recommend replacing silver amalgam fillings if they are cracked, leaking, or showing signs of decay underneath. Many patients also choose to replace them for aesthetic reasons. Our dentists will safely remove the old amalgam and replace it with a perfectly matched composite restoration.

Will the filling stain over time?

Premium nanohybrid composites resist staining quite well. However, over many years, some surface staining may occur with heavy consumption of coffee, tea, or red wine. Regular dental cleanings and polishing help maintain the filling's original colour and shine.
