Frequently Asked Questions

Common signs include bleeding gums during brushing, persistent bad breath, swollen or red gums, receding gums, and loose teeth. If you notice any of these symptoms, schedule a periodontal evaluation immediately.

Laser gum treatments are significantly less painful than traditional surgery. The laser cauterises tissue as it works, reducing bleeding and discomfort. Most patients report minimal pain and require only over-the-counter pain relief afterwards.

We recommend professional scaling and polishing every 6 months for healthy gums. If you have a history of gum disease, your periodontist may advise more frequent cleanings every 3-4 months to keep the condition under control.
