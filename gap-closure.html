Gap Closure with Veneers & Crowns

Close unsightly gaps between your teeth seamlessly with custom-crafted porcelain veneers and all-ceramic crowns for a perfectly aligned, natural-looking smile.

About This Treatment

Close the Gap, Perfect Your Smile

Diastema, or gaps between teeth, is one of the most common cosmetic concerns patients bring to us. Whether it is a single prominent gap between your front teeth or multiple spaces throughout your smile, our cosmetic specialists offer fast, predictable solutions using porcelain veneers or all-ceramic crowns.

Unlike orthodontic treatment which can take months, gap closure with veneers is typically completed in just two visits over 5–7 days. The veneers are custom-shaped and shaded to create a seamless, natural appearance that blends perfectly with the rest of your teeth.

  • Instant results in just 2 visits
  • No braces or aligners required
  • Custom shade matching for natural look
  • Long-lasting E.max porcelain veneers
  • Minimal tooth preparation needed
How It Works

The Gap Closure Process

1

Assessment & Planning

Thorough examination and digital imaging to assess gap size, tooth structure, and determine the best approach — veneers, crowns, or composite bonding.

2

Tooth Preparation

Minimal enamel preparation of the teeth adjacent to the gap. Precision impressions or digital scans are taken for the ceramics laboratory.

3

Custom Fabrication

Your veneers or crowns are hand-crafted in our dental lab from premium E.max or zirconia ceramics, matched to your exact shade and dimensions.

4

Bonding & Finishing

Final veneers are permanently bonded with precision. Bite adjustments and polishing ensure perfect comfort and a flawless, gap-free smile.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can all gaps be closed with veneers?

Most small to moderate gaps (up to 3–4 mm) can be effectively closed with porcelain veneers. For larger gaps, a combination of orthodontics and veneers, or crowns may be recommended. Our specialists will assess your specific situation during consultation.

How long do gap closure veneers last?

Porcelain veneers used for gap closure typically last 10–15 years with proper care. They are stain-resistant and maintain their colour over time. Regular dental check-ups and good oral hygiene ensure maximum longevity.

Will the result look natural?

Absolutely. Our veneers are handcrafted from translucent porcelain that mimics the light-reflecting properties of natural enamel. Each veneer is custom-shaded to match your surrounding teeth, making the result virtually indistinguishable from natural teeth.
