Pricing & Payment FAQs

Everything you need to know about our charges, payment options, and insurance.

After an in-clinic or video consultation, we provide a detailed, itemized cost sheet with no hidden fees.
Yes. For treatments above ₹20,000, we offer no-cost EMI options through select partner banks (HDFC, ICICI, SBI, Bajaj Finserv). You can split your bill into 3, 6, 9, or 12 monthly installments. We also accept phased payments aligned with multi-visit treatment schedules.
