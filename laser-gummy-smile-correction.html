What Is a Gummy Smile?

A "gummy smile" occurs when an excessive amount of gum tissue is visible above the upper teeth when you smile. This can make teeth appear shorter than they actually are and cause self-consciousness. Research shows that a smile is considered "gummy" when more than 3-4mm of gum tissue is visible.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, our periodontists use advanced laser crown lengthening to precisely sculpt the gum line, revealing more of your natural tooth structure and creating a beautifully balanced, symmetrical smile. The procedure is completed in a single visit with immediate visible results.

The Correction Process

1

Smile Analysis

Digital photographs and measurements determine the ideal gum line position for your unique facial proportions and tooth anatomy.

2

Gum Line Mapping

The periodontist carefully marks the new gum line ensuring perfect symmetry across all visible teeth in your smile zone.

3

Laser Sculpting

A precision diode laser gently reshapes the gum tissue, simultaneously cauterising to minimise bleeding and promote healing.

4

Reveal Your Smile

Your new smile is instantly visible. Simple aftercare instructions ensure optimal healing over the following 7-10 days.
