What Is Laser Dentistry?

Laser dentistry uses concentrated beams of light energy to treat gum disease, reshape soft tissue, and eliminate bacteria with unmatched precision. At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we use state-of-the-art diode and Er:YAG lasers to perform procedures that were once only possible through traditional surgery.

The laser's ability to target diseased tissue while leaving healthy tissue untouched means significantly less bleeding, reduced swelling, and dramatically faster healing. Most patients experience little to no discomfort during and after the procedure, often eliminating the need for sutures entirely.

Virtually painless — minimal anaesthesia needed
70% faster healing compared to traditional surgery
No scalpel, no sutures, minimal bleeding
Precise bacterial elimination at the source
FDA-approved and clinically proven technology
Same-day return to normal activities

How Laser Gum Treatment Works

1

Digital Diagnosis

We perform a thorough periodontal evaluation with digital X-rays and probing to map pocket depths and disease severity.

2

Laser Application

The calibrated laser targets infected gum tissue and bacteria in periodontal pockets without affecting surrounding healthy tissue.

3

Debris Removal

Ultrasonic instruments remove calculus and debris from root surfaces while the laser continues to sterilise the treatment area.

4

Tissue Regeneration

The laser stimulates a blood clot that promotes natural tissue reattachment and bone regeneration around the treated teeth.
