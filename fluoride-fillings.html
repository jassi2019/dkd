Protective, tooth-coloured fillings that restore your child's smile safely and gently

Home Treatments Kids Dentistry Fluoride Fillings

What Are Fluoride Fillings?

Fluoride fillings, also known as glass ionomer cement (GIC) fillings, are specially designed restorative materials that release fluoride over time. They are ideal for children because they not only repair cavities but actively protect the surrounding tooth structure from further decay.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we use high-quality, BPA-free, tooth-coloured filling materials that bond chemically with your child's tooth. The entire procedure is gentle, quick, and often completed in a single visit with minimal discomfort.

Benefits of Fluoride Fillings

Continuous fluoride release prevents recurrent decay
Tooth-coloured, aesthetically pleasing results
BPA-free, biocompatible materials safe for children
Minimal tooth preparation required
Chemical bond to tooth structure for better seal
Completed in a single, short appointment

The Fluoride Filling Process

1

Gentle Examination

Our pediatric dentist examines the affected tooth and takes a digital X-ray to assess the extent of decay.

2

Painless Preparation

Using child-friendly anaesthesia techniques, the decayed portion is gently removed with minimal drilling.

3

Filling Placement

The fluoride-releasing filling material is carefully placed, shaped, and bonded to the tooth surface.

4

Polish & Check

The filling is polished for a smooth finish and the bite is checked to ensure your child's comfort.
