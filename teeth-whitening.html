About This Treatment

A Brighter Smile in One Visit

Professional teeth whitening is the fastest, safest, and most effective way to brighten your smile. Unlike over-the-counter products that deliver inconsistent results, our in-office whitening uses clinically proven, high-concentration gels activated by specialised LED light to break down deep stains and discolouration.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we offer both in-office power whitening for instant results and customised take-home whitening trays for gradual brightening at your convenience. Our dentists carefully assess your teeth before treatment to determine the best approach, ensuring safe, even whitening with minimal sensitivity.

  • Both in-office and take-home options available

How It Works

The Whitening Process

1

Dental Examination

Your dentist examines your teeth for cavities, gum health, and existing restorations. A professional cleaning removes surface plaque and tartar for even whitening.

2

Gum Protection

A protective barrier is applied to shield your gums and soft tissues from the whitening gel, ensuring complete comfort throughout the procedure.

3

Whitening Application

Professional-grade hydrogen peroxide gel is applied to your teeth and activated with our LED light system. Multiple cycles achieve optimal brightness.

4

Reveal & Aftercare

The gel is removed to reveal your dramatically whiter smile. We provide aftercare instructions and optional take-home trays for maintenance.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is professional whitening safe for my teeth?

Yes, professional teeth whitening supervised by a qualified dentist is completely safe. The gels we use are clinically tested and approved. Unlike unregulated products, professional whitening is carefully controlled to protect your enamel and gum tissue.

How long do whitening results last?

Professional whitening results typically last 1–2 years. The longevity depends on your diet and habits. Avoiding heavy staining from coffee, tea, red wine, and tobacco helps maintain brightness. Periodic touch-ups with take-home trays can extend results further.

Will I experience sensitivity after whitening?

Some patients experience mild, temporary sensitivity for 24–48 hours after treatment. We use desensitising agents before and after the procedure to minimise this. Our sensitivity-free whitening formulas are also available for patients with naturally sensitive teeth.
