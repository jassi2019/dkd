What Are Dental Crowns & Bridges?

A dental crown is a tooth-shaped cap placed over a damaged or weakened tooth to restore its shape, size, strength, and appearance. A bridge is a fixed prosthetic that replaces one or more missing teeth by anchoring to adjacent natural teeth or implants, literally "bridging" the gap in your smile.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we offer the finest crown and bridge materials including Zirconia, E-max lithium disilicate, and porcelain fused to metal options. Our in-house CAD/CAM technology and collaboration with premium dental laboratories ensure restorations that are virtually indistinguishable from natural teeth.

The Crown & Bridge Process

Precision-crafted restorations delivered in just two visits

1

Consultation & Planning

Thorough examination with digital X-rays and intraoral scans to assess the tooth condition. We discuss material options and create a personalized treatment plan.

2

Tooth Preparation

The tooth is carefully shaped to accommodate the crown. Precise digital impressions are taken and a temporary crown is placed for protection during fabrication.

3

Lab Fabrication

Your custom crown or bridge is crafted using CAD/CAM technology with premium materials, colour-matched to blend seamlessly with your natural teeth.

4

Fitting & Cementation

The final restoration is tried in, adjusted for perfect bite and aesthetics, then permanently cemented. You leave with a strong, beautiful, fully functional tooth.
