Instant Smile Makeover

A complete smile transformation combining porcelain veneers, crowns, and professional whitening — designed, crafted, and delivered in as few as 3–5 days.

About This Treatment

Your Dream Smile, Designed Digitally

An Instant Smile Makeover is a comprehensive cosmetic procedure that addresses multiple aesthetic concerns in a single coordinated treatment plan. Our specialists use Digital Smile Design (DSD) technology to preview your new smile before any treatment begins, ensuring you know exactly what to expect.

Using a combination of ultra-thin porcelain veneers, all-ceramic crowns, gum contouring, and professional whitening, we craft a smile that is perfectly proportioned, brilliantly white, and uniquely suited to your facial features. International patients frequently visit us for this procedure, completing their entire smile transformation within a week.

  • Digital Smile Design preview before treatment
  • International-grade E.max and zirconia materials
  • Complete transformation in 3–5 days
  • Natural-looking, long-lasting results (10–15 years)
  • Painless procedure with local anaesthesia

How It Works

The Smile Makeover Process

1

Consultation & Digital Design

Comprehensive examination, photographs, 3D scans, and Digital Smile Design to preview your new smile on screen before we begin.

2

Tooth Preparation

Minimal tooth preparation under local anaesthesia. Precise impressions are sent to our in-house ceramics lab for custom fabrication.

3

Trial & Adjustments

A trial smile is placed so you can see and approve the shape, shade, and fit before final bonding. Adjustments are made for perfection.

4

Final Bonding & Reveal

Permanent veneers or crowns are bonded using advanced adhesive systems. Your stunning new smile is revealed — ready to dazzle.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a smile makeover last?

With proper care and regular dental check-ups, porcelain veneers and crowns used in a smile makeover typically last 10 to 15 years. Some patients enjoy their results for even longer. We use only international-grade materials like E.max and zirconia for maximum durability.

Is the smile makeover procedure painful?

Not at all. The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia, so you feel no pain during treatment. Most patients report only mild sensitivity for a day or two afterward, which resolves quickly. We prioritise your comfort throughout the process.

Can I see my new smile before committing?

Absolutely! We use Digital Smile Design technology to create a virtual preview of your new smile before any treatment begins. You can also get a trial smile (mock-up) placed temporarily so you can see and feel the result before final bonding.
