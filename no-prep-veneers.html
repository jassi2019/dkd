No-Prep / Prepless Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded directly to your teeth without any grinding or drilling — the most conservative path to a flawless smile.

About This Treatment

Beautiful Smiles, Zero Grinding

No-Prep Veneers, also known as prepless veneers, represent the most conservative approach in cosmetic dentistry. These wafer-thin porcelain laminates — as thin as 0.3 mm — are bonded directly over your existing teeth without the need for any enamel removal or drilling.

This revolutionary technique is ideal for patients who want to enhance their smile without any irreversible changes to their natural tooth structure. The procedure is completely pain-free, requires no anaesthesia, and delivers stunning results that look and feel entirely natural. Each veneer is individually handcrafted by our master ceramists to ensure seamless integration with your smile.

  • No drilling, grinding, or enamel removal
  • Completely painless — no anaesthesia needed
  • Reversible procedure preserving natural teeth
  • Ultra-thin 0.3 mm porcelain laminates
  • Completed in just 2 comfortable visits
Advantages

Why Choose No-Prep Veneers?

  • Preserve 100% of your natural tooth enamel
  • No temporary veneers needed between visits
  • Stain-resistant, durable porcelain material
  • Correct minor chips, gaps, and discolouration
  • Can be removed later if desired (reversible)
  • Natural translucency matches real teeth

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between no-prep and traditional veneers?

Traditional veneers require removing a thin layer of enamel (0.5–0.7 mm) before placement, making the process irreversible. No-prep veneers are much thinner (0.3 mm) and bonded directly to your teeth without any grinding. This preserves your natural tooth structure and makes the procedure completely reversible.

Am I a good candidate for no-prep veneers?

No-prep veneers are ideal for patients with minor cosmetic issues such as slight discolouration, small gaps, minor chips, or teeth that need lengthening. If you have severely crooked, crowded, or protruding teeth, traditional veneers or orthodontics may be more appropriate. We assess each case individually.

How long do no-prep veneers last?

With proper oral hygiene and regular dental visits, no-prep veneers typically last 7–12 years. They are made from high-quality porcelain that resists staining and wear. Avoiding habits like biting hard objects helps maximise their lifespan.
