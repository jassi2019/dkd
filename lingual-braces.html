What Are Lingual Braces?

Lingual braces are custom-made orthodontic brackets that are bonded to the inner (tongue-side) surfaces of your teeth, making them completely invisible from the outside. They provide the full corrective power of traditional braces while maintaining a completely natural appearance throughout your treatment.

Ideal for professionals, performers, and anyone who wants to straighten their teeth discreetly, lingual braces at Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry are custom-fabricated using CAD/CAM technology for a precise fit and efficient tooth movement. Our experienced orthodontists have successfully treated hundreds of patients with lingual systems.

Benefits of Lingual Braces

100% invisible from the outside
Custom-fabricated for precise, comfortable fit
Effective for complex orthodontic cases
No dietary restrictions (unlike aligners, always working)
CAD/CAM technology for optimal results
Ideal for professionals and public-facing individuals

Your Lingual Braces Journey

1

Consultation & 3D Scan

Detailed examination, digital impressions, and 3D scans create a precise model for custom bracket fabrication.

2

Custom Fabrication

Your brackets are custom-manufactured using CAD/CAM technology to fit the unique contours of each tooth's inner surface.

3

Bonding & Activation

Brackets are precisely bonded to the lingual surfaces using indirect bonding trays for accurate placement.

4

Adjustments & Results

Regular adjustments every 4-6 weeks progressively align your teeth. Retainers maintain your beautiful results.

Frequently Asked Questions

You may notice slight changes in speech for the first 1-2 weeks as your tongue adjusts to the brackets. Most patients adapt quickly, and any speech differences become unnoticeable. Practising reading aloud can help speed up the adjustment period.

Initial tongue irritation is common for the first week but subsides as your mouth adapts. Modern lingual brackets are significantly smaller and smoother than earlier versions, greatly improving comfort. Dental wax can also be used during the adjustment period.

Treatment duration is comparable to conventional braces, typically 12-24 months depending on the complexity of your case. Because the brackets are custom-made for precision, tooth movement can actually be very efficient with lingual systems.
