Advanced Dental Technology

We invest in the world's most advanced dental equipment so every treatment is faster, more precise, and more comfortable for you.

Our Equipment

Cutting-Edge Technology

Every piece of equipment in our clinic is chosen for accuracy, patient comfort, and clinical excellence.

CEREC CAD/CAM

Same-day ceramic crowns, veneers, and inlays designed and milled chairside using computer-aided design and manufacturing. No more temporary restorations or multiple visits — your permanent restoration is ready in under 90 minutes.

3D CBCT Scanner

Our Cone Beam Computed Tomography scanner generates detailed 3D images of your jaw, teeth, nerves, and sinuses. Essential for precise implant planning, root canal diagnosis, and surgical procedures with up to 90% less radiation.

Dental Operating Microscope

Our Carl Zeiss surgical microscopes provide up to 25x magnification, enabling our endodontists to locate hidden canals, detect micro-fractures, and perform ultra-precise root canal treatments with exceptional success rates.

Diode Lasers

Soft tissue diode lasers for painless gum recontouring, frenectomy, and periodontal therapy. Laser treatments mean minimal bleeding, faster healing, reduced post-operative discomfort, and no need for sutures in most cases.

Digital Smile Design

See your new smile before treatment begins. Our DSD software creates a digital mock-up of your ideal smile based on facial proportions, lip dynamics, and your personal preferences — so you approve the design before we start.

Autoclave Sterilization

Class B vacuum autoclaves sterilize every instrument at 134°C under high pressure. Combined with ultrasonic cleaning baths and weekly biological spore testing, our sterilization protocols exceed NABH and international standards.

In Detail

How Our Technology Benefits You

CEREC: Same-Day Crowns

Traditional crowns require two visits and a temporary crown for weeks. With CEREC, we digitally scan your tooth, design the restoration on screen, and mill it from a solid block of ceramic — all in a single appointment.

The result is a perfectly colour-matched, precisely fitting ceramic restoration that bonds permanently to your tooth the same day. No impressions, no temporaries, no second visit.

3D CBCT: Precision Imaging

A single 14-second scan produces a complete 3D model of your oral anatomy. Our implantologists use this data to plan exact implant placement, angles, and depth — ensuring predictable, safe surgical outcomes.

For complex root canal cases, the CBCT reveals hidden anatomy that conventional X-rays miss, dramatically improving treatment success rates.
