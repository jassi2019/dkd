Tooth Crystal / Tooth Jewelry

Add a dazzling accent to your smile with premium dental crystals and gems — safe, painless, and completely removable whenever you choose.

About This Treatment

Sparkle When You Smile

Tooth crystals and dental jewelry are a fun, fashionable way to add personality to your smile. These small, high-quality crystals are bonded to the surface of your tooth using the same safe adhesive technology used in orthodontics. The procedure is completely painless, takes just 15–20 minutes, and requires no drilling or alteration to your tooth.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we offer a range of premium tooth gems including Swarovski crystals, sapphire-cut gems, and even genuine diamond accents. Whether you want a subtle twinkle or a bold statement piece, our cosmetic dentists will help you choose the perfect crystal and placement for your unique smile.

  • Completely painless — no drilling required
  • Quick 15–20 minute procedure
  • Removable without any damage to the tooth
  • Premium Swarovski and diamond options
  • Lasts 6 months to 2 years depending on care
How It Works

The Application Process

1

Crystal Selection

Browse our collection and choose from clear crystals, coloured gems, or diamond accents. We help you select the size and placement that suits your smile best.

2

Tooth Preparation

The tooth surface is gently cleaned and conditioned with a mild etching agent. No drilling or enamel removal is involved — just surface preparation for bonding.

3

Crystal Bonding

Using dental-grade adhesive, the crystal is precisely positioned and permanently bonded. A curing light secures the bond in seconds.

4

Shine & Go

The area is polished and you are given aftercare instructions. Walk out with a sparkling new addition to your smile in under 20 minutes.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is tooth jewelry safe for my teeth?

Yes, tooth jewelry applied by a professional dentist is completely safe. The crystal is bonded using the same adhesive used for orthodontic brackets, which has decades of proven safety. No drilling or enamel removal is required, and the crystal can be removed at any time without damage to the tooth.

How long does a tooth crystal last?

A professionally applied tooth crystal typically lasts 6 months to 2 years, depending on your oral hygiene habits, diet, and placement location. Avoid biting hard foods directly on the crystal. If it falls off, it can easily be re-bonded at a follow-up visit.

Can I brush my teeth normally with a tooth crystal?

Absolutely! You can brush and floss normally with a tooth crystal. In fact, good oral hygiene helps the crystal stay cleaner and last longer. Avoid using electric toothbrush heads directly on the crystal, and brush gently around the bonded area.
