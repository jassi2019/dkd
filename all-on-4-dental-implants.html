What Are All-on-4 Dental Implants?

The All-on-4 technique is a revolutionary implant protocol that replaces an entire arch of missing teeth using just four strategically positioned implants. Two implants are placed vertically in the front of the jaw, and two are angled at up to 45 degrees in the back, maximising bone contact without the need for bone grafting.

The most remarkable advantage is immediate function — a full set of fixed temporary teeth is attached to the implants on the same day as surgery. You walk into our clinic with missing or failing teeth and walk out with a complete, beautiful smile in a single appointment.

Benefits of All-on-4 Implants

Full arch of teeth in just one day — immediate results
Only 4 implants needed — fewer surgeries, lower cost
Usually no bone grafting required even with bone loss
Fixed teeth — no removable dentures, no adhesives
Restores full chewing capacity and natural speech
Proven 95%+ long-term success rate worldwide

The Treatment Process

Your journey from missing teeth to a full smile in one day

1

Digital Planning

3D CBCT scans and digital modelling are used to virtually plan every implant position. A surgical guide is fabricated for pinpoint accuracy during the procedure.

2

Implant Placement

Four titanium implants are placed under local anaesthesia or IV sedation. Two straight in the anterior and two tilted in the posterior, using available bone for maximum anchorage.

3

Same-Day Teeth

A pre-fabricated fixed bridge with a full set of teeth is attached to the implants the same day. You leave the clinic with a functional, aesthetic smile immediately.

4

Final Prosthesis

After 3-6 months of healing and osseointegration, the temporary bridge is replaced with your final, precision-milled zirconia or acrylic prosthesis for a lifetime of confident smiles.
