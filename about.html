Our Story

Where World-Class Dentistry Meets Compassionate Care

Founded in 2006, Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry has grown from a single-chair practice to one of India's most trusted multi-speciality dental centres. Located at E-143, East of Kailash, New Delhi, our NABH-accredited facility is equipped with the latest dental technology from Europe and the USA.

What started as a vision by Dr. Sween Kathuria and Dr. Puneet Kathuria has evolved into a team of 9 specialist dentists, each a leader in their field. We have served over 15,000 patients from 44+ countries — from Hollywood smile makeovers and full-mouth implant rehabilitations to complex root canal therapies and gum surgeries.

Our clinic follows ADA protocols and maintains an academic tie-up with the University of Louisville, USA, ensuring every treatment meets the highest international benchmarks.

Advanced Technology

State-of-the-Art Equipment

We invest in the latest dental technology to ensure precise diagnostics, comfortable treatments, and predictable outcomes.

CEREC CAD/CAM

Same-day ceramic crowns, veneers, and inlays milled chairside with digital precision — no messy impressions, no temporary restorations, no second visit.

3D CBCT Scanner

Cone Beam Computed Tomography provides detailed 3D imaging of teeth, bone, and nerves — essential for accurate implant planning and complex surgical cases.

Dental Lasers

Diode and Er:YAG lasers for painless gum surgery, cavity preparation, and teeth whitening. Minimally invasive procedures with faster healing and less discomfort.

Digital Smile Design

AI-powered smile simulation lets you preview your new smile before treatment begins. Plan with confidence using high-resolution digital mockups and 3D models.
