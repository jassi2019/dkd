Our Credentials

We hold the highest quality certifications in healthcare, ensuring every patient receives safe, standardised, and world-class dental care.

Our Accreditations

Certified to International Standards

Each accreditation represents rigorous third-party evaluation of our facilities, processes, and patient outcomes.

India's Gold Standard

NABH Accreditation

The National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH) is India's highest quality and patient safety benchmark for healthcare facilities. It is a constituent board of the Quality Council of India, set up to establish and operate accreditation programmes for healthcare organisations.

Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry is one of only a handful of standalone dental clinics in Delhi to achieve this prestigious accreditation, which requires compliance with over 600 measurable standards covering patient rights, infection control, clinical outcomes, and facility management.

What This Means for You

  • Standardised clinical protocols for every procedure
  • Documented infection control and sterilisation practices
  • Trained staff with ongoing quality education
  • Transparent patient rights and grievance redressal
  • Regular audits ensuring sustained compliance
International Standard

ISO 9001:2015

The International Organisation for Standardisation's ISO 9001:2015 certification confirms that our quality management system meets global benchmarks. This internationally recognised standard evaluates how well an organisation manages its processes, documentation, and continuous improvement initiatives.

Our certification covers every aspect of clinic operations — from appointment scheduling and patient records to instrument sterilisation and treatment follow-up. The ISO framework ensures we deliver consistently high-quality care that improves over time.

What This Means for You

  • Globally recognised quality management system
  • Documented, repeatable processes for consistent care
  • Continuous improvement through regular internal audits
  • Reliable patient records management and data security
  • Systematic approach to patient feedback and resolution
Laboratory Standards

NABL Standards

The National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL) sets the benchmark for accuracy and reliability in diagnostic testing. Our in-house diagnostic processes, including digital X-rays, CBCT scans, and laboratory workflows, follow NABL-compliant standards.

This ensures that every diagnostic image, scan report, and lab result you receive from our clinic is accurate, calibrated, and reliable — forming the foundation for precise treatment planning and successful outcomes.

What This Means for You

  • Accurate and calibrated diagnostic equipment
  • Reliable imaging results for precise treatment planning
  • Regular equipment calibration and quality checks
  • Traceable and documented diagnostic workflows
  • Confidence in every scan and report issued
