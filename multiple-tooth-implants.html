Restore several missing teeth with strong, implant-supported bridges

What Are Multiple Teeth Implants?

When you are missing two or more adjacent teeth, multiple teeth implants offer an efficient and cost-effective solution. Instead of placing one implant per missing tooth, two or more implants are strategically positioned to support a fixed bridge that spans the entire gap.

This approach provides maximum stability with fewer implant posts, reducing both treatment time and cost. The implant-supported bridge distributes chewing forces evenly across the jaw, preserving bone health and restoring full function.

Benefits of Multiple Teeth Implants

Fewer implants needed than one per tooth — cost-effective
Fixed, non-removable restoration — no slipping or clicking
Preserves jawbone and prevents facial sagging
Restores full chewing power for all types of food
Natural appearance that blends with existing teeth
No damage to surrounding healthy teeth

The Treatment Process

A proven four-step approach to restoring your smile

1

Assessment & Planning

Detailed 3D CBCT scans and digital impressions map your jawbone anatomy. Our implantologist designs a precise treatment plan with optimal implant positions.

2

Implant Surgery

Two or more titanium implant posts are placed into the jawbone at strategic locations under local anaesthesia. The procedure typically takes 60-90 minutes.

3

Osseointegration

Over 3-6 months, the implants fuse with the jawbone. You wear a temporary bridge during this healing phase so your smile remains complete.

4

Bridge Placement

Once integration is confirmed, your custom-made porcelain bridge is securely attached, restoring aesthetics and full biting function permanently.
