FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use the card for all family members at once?

Yes! The card covers up to 4 family members (any combination of adults and children). All members can use the card simultaneously for different appointments. Additional members can be added for ₹1,000 each.

Does the 10% discount apply to implants and cosmetic treatments?

Absolutely. The 10% discount applies to every treatment we offer, including dental implants, smile makeovers, veneers, orthodontics, and cosmetic procedures. There are no exclusions.

Can the card be renewed? What happens after one year?

The card is valid for one year from the date of enrollment. You can renew it at the same ₹5,000/year rate. We'll send you a reminder 30 days before expiration. Loyal members may receive special renewal discounts.

Is the privilege card transferable?

The card is linked to the registered family members and is not transferable to others. However, you can update family member names once during the card's validity period by contacting our front desk.

Can I combine the card discount with other offers?

The privilege card discount cannot be combined with other promotional offers or seasonal discounts. However, the card discount is guaranteed and available year-round, making it more reliable than time-limited promotions.
