What Are Fluoride Applications?

Fluoride application is a quick, painless preventive treatment where a concentrated fluoride varnish is painted directly onto your child's teeth. The fluoride penetrates the enamel, strengthening it against acid attacks from bacteria and sugary foods, and can even reverse the earliest stages of tooth decay.

Recommended by the American Academy of Pediatric Dentistry every six months, professional fluoride applications provide significantly higher protection than fluoride toothpaste alone. At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we use FDA-approved, child-safe fluoride varnishes that set on contact and taste pleasant for young patients.

Benefits of Professional Fluoride

Reduces cavity risk by up to 40%
Reverses early-stage white spot lesions
Strengthens developing enamel in children
Completely painless, takes under 5 minutes
FDA-approved, pleasant-tasting varnish
Safe for children from age 1 onwards

Simple 4-Step Process

1

Oral Examination

Our dentist checks your child's teeth for any signs of decay, plaque buildup, or areas needing extra protection.

2

Teeth Cleaning

Teeth are gently cleaned and dried to ensure the fluoride varnish adheres properly to the enamel surface.

3

Varnish Application

A thin layer of fluoride varnish is painted onto all tooth surfaces using a soft brush. It sets instantly on contact with saliva.

4

Aftercare Advice

Simple instructions are provided: avoid hot foods for 4 hours and brush normally the next morning. That's it!

For most children, we recommend professional fluoride application every six months during their routine dental check-up. Children at higher risk of cavities (due to diet, brushing habits, or medical conditions) may benefit from quarterly applications as advised by the dentist.

Fluoride varnish can be safely applied as soon as the first tooth erupts, typically around 6 months of age. Early application is especially beneficial for children in families with a history of cavities or those whose water supply is not fluoridated.
