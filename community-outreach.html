Our Programs

Community Initiatives

From rural dental camps to urban school programs, we are committed to improving oral health across communities.

50+ camps conducted across Delhi NCR

Free Dental Camps

Our doctors and dental hygienists regularly organise free dental check-up camps in underserved areas across Delhi NCR and neighbouring states. These camps provide free oral screenings, basic treatments, fluoride applications, and dental health education at absolutely no cost to participants.

Each camp is equipped with portable dental equipment, sterilised instruments, and educational materials in Hindi and English. Patients requiring advanced treatments receive subsidised referrals to our clinic.

  • 50+ free dental camps conducted since 2008
  • Over 5,000 free dental screenings provided
  • Camps in rural villages, urban slums, and community centres
  • Free fluoride treatment and basic dental care
100% free — no charges for camp attendees
20+ schools in Delhi NCR

School Dental Programs

Prevention starts with education. Our school outreach program visits government and private schools across Delhi NCR to teach children proper brushing techniques, the importance of dental hygiene, and the effects of sugar on teeth — all through fun, interactive sessions.

Each child receives a free dental screening, a toothbrush kit, and an oral hygiene report card sent home to parents. Children identified with urgent dental issues are offered subsidised treatment at our clinic.

  • 20+ schools visited with interactive dental workshops
  • Free toothbrush kits distributed to all students
  • Oral hygiene report cards sent to parents
  • Subsidised treatment for children needing care
Teaching healthy habits from an early age
Free check-ups for senior citizens

Senior Citizen Free Check-ups

We believe age should never be a barrier to dental health. Our senior citizen program offers complimentary dental consultations and check-ups for individuals aged 60 and above at our clinic every month.

Many elderly patients suffer from issues like loose dentures, gum disease, and difficulty chewing that significantly impact their quality of life. Our team provides compassionate, gentle care tailored to the unique needs of older adults.

  • Monthly free check-up days for seniors 60+
  • Free X-rays and oral cancer screening
  • Discounted dentures and implant consultations
  • Gentle, patient-centred approach for elderly patients
1,200+ senior citizens served to date
