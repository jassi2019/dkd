Facility Highlights

Designed for Comfort & Precision

The Waiting Area

Our lounge is designed to put you at ease the moment you walk in. Comfortable seating, calming interiors, complimentary beverages, and free Wi-Fi ensure a relaxing experience.

  • Climate-controlled environment
  • Complimentary tea, coffee & water
  • Free high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Digital entertainment displays

Treatment Rooms

Each of our six treatment rooms is equipped with world-class dental chairs, overhead LED surgical lights, intraoral cameras, and ceiling-mounted entertainment screens for patient comfort.

  • Six fully equipped operatories
  • HEPA air filtration systems
  • Intraoral cameras for live viewing
  • Ceiling-mounted patient TVs

Sterilization Room

Our NABH-compliant sterilization room follows a strict unidirectional workflow. Every instrument passes through ultrasonic cleaning, Class B autoclaving, and biological spore testing.

  • Class B autoclave sterilization
  • Ultrasonic instrument cleaning
  • Weekly biological spore testing
  • Colour-coded waste management

X-Ray Suite

Our dedicated imaging suite houses a 3D CBCT scanner and digital OPG unit. All imaging is digital, providing instant high-resolution results with up to 90% lower radiation than traditional film X-rays.

  • 3D CBCT cone beam scanner
  • Digital OPG panoramic unit
  • 90% lower radiation exposure
  • Instant results — no waiting
