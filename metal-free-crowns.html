Metal Free Crowns & Bridges

All-ceramic zirconia and E.max crowns that deliver unmatched aesthetics, strength, and biocompatibility — no dark metal lines, no allergies, just pure natural beauty.

About This Treatment

The Future of Dental Crowns

Metal-free crowns and bridges represent the gold standard in modern restorative dentistry. Made entirely from advanced ceramics like zirconia and lithium disilicate (E.max), these restorations eliminate the dark grey line at the gum margin that is common with traditional metal-ceramic (PFM) crowns.

Zirconia crowns are incredibly strong — matching the strength of metal while delivering the translucency and lifelike appearance of natural teeth. They are 100% biocompatible, making them ideal for patients with metal allergies or sensitivity. Whether you need a single crown after a root canal, multiple crowns for a smile makeover, or a bridge to replace missing teeth, all-ceramic restorations provide the best combination of aesthetics and durability available today.

  • No dark metal lines at the gum margin
  • Natural translucency mimics real teeth
  • 100% biocompatible — no metal allergies
  • Exceptional strength for front and back teeth
  • CAD/CAM precision for perfect fit

How It Works

The Crown Process

1

Consultation & Planning

Comprehensive assessment of the tooth, shade selection, and discussion of crown material options (zirconia, E.max, or layered zirconia) best suited for your case.

2

Tooth Preparation

The tooth is carefully shaped under local anaesthesia. Precise digital impressions capture every detail for the laboratory. A temporary crown protects the tooth.

3

CAD/CAM Fabrication

Your crown is designed digitally and milled from a solid block of zirconia or E.max ceramic using computer-aided manufacturing for unmatched precision.

4

Cementation & Finishing

The final crown is tried in for fit, colour, and bite accuracy. Once approved, it is permanently cemented. Your smile is restored with a natural, metal-free tooth.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between zirconia and E.max crowns?

Zirconia crowns are extremely strong and ideal for back teeth (molars and premolars) where bite forces are highest. E.max (lithium disilicate) crowns offer superior translucency and are preferred for front teeth where aesthetics are paramount. For the best of both, layered zirconia crowns combine a strong zirconia core with a hand-layered porcelain exterior.

How long do metal-free crowns last?

With proper oral hygiene and regular dental check-ups, zirconia and E.max crowns typically last 15–20 years or more. Many patients enjoy their metal-free crowns for a lifetime. Unlike PFM crowns, there is no porcelain chipping from metal because the entire crown is a single ceramic structure.

Should I replace my existing metal crowns?

If your existing metal or PFM crowns are showing dark lines at the gum margin, causing allergic reactions, or are aesthetically displeasing, replacing them with metal-free alternatives is an excellent option. Many patients choose to upgrade when their existing crowns reach the end of their lifespan. Our dentists will assess whether replacement is beneficial for your situation.
