What Is a Single Tooth Implant?

A single tooth implant is a standalone replacement for one missing tooth. It consists of three components: a titanium implant post that fuses with your jawbone, an abutment connector, and a custom-crafted porcelain crown that matches your natural teeth perfectly.

Unlike a traditional bridge that requires grinding down adjacent healthy teeth, a single implant preserves your surrounding tooth structure while preventing bone loss at the extraction site. The result is a restoration that is virtually indistinguishable from your natural teeth.

Benefits of Single Tooth Implants

Preserves adjacent healthy teeth — no grinding needed
Prevents jawbone deterioration at the missing tooth site
Looks and feels exactly like a natural tooth
Easy to clean — brush and floss normally
98.5% success rate at our clinic
Can last a lifetime with proper care

The Treatment Process

Your journey to a complete smile in four simple steps

1

Consultation & 3D Scan

Comprehensive oral examination with CBCT 3D imaging to evaluate bone density, plan implant position, and discuss your treatment options.

2

Implant Placement

The titanium implant post is precisely placed into the jawbone under local anaesthesia. The procedure typically takes 30-45 minutes with minimal discomfort.

3

Healing & Integration

Over 3-6 months, the implant fuses with your jawbone through osseointegration. A temporary crown keeps your smile intact during this period.

4

Crown Placement

Once fully integrated, a custom porcelain crown is fabricated and attached to the implant, giving you a permanent, beautiful tooth.
