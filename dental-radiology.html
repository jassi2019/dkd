Advanced Digital Dental Imaging

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective dental treatment. At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we use the latest digital radiography technology that produces crystal-clear images instantly while exposing patients to up to 90% less radiation compared to traditional film-based X-rays.

Our in-house radiology suite is equipped with digital RVG sensors for detailed intraoral imaging and a digital panoramic (OPG) machine for comprehensive full-mouth views. All images are stored digitally for easy sharing with specialists and for tracking treatment progress over time.

Why Our Imaging Is Superior

The radiation dose from a digital dental X-ray is extremely low — less than the background radiation you receive in a single day from natural sources. We also use lead aprons and thyroid collars for added protection.

Most patients benefit from bitewing X-rays annually and a full panoramic X-ray every 3-5 years. Your dentist may recommend more frequent imaging if you are undergoing treatment for gum disease, implant planning, or root canal therapy. We follow the ALARA principle — as low as reasonably achievable.

While digital dental X-rays involve minimal radiation, we generally postpone non-urgent X-rays during pregnancy as a precaution. If an X-ray is medically necessary for emergency treatment, we use additional shielding and the lowest possible exposure settings to ensure safety.
