What Are Nobel Biocare Implants?

Nobel Biocare is the world's leading dental implant manufacturer, headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland. Founded on the pioneering work of Professor Per-Ingvar Branemark — the father of modern implantology — Nobel Biocare has over 30 years of clinical research and more than 2,000 published scientific studies supporting their implant systems.

At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, we are an authorised Nobel Biocare centre. Every Nobel Biocare implant we place comes with a unique serial number, a global warranty, and a patient passport that ensures your implant can be identified and serviced by any Nobel Biocare-trained dentist anywhere in the world.

The Treatment Process

Premium implant care from consultation to final restoration

1

Consultation & Implant Selection

After a thorough examination and 3D scans, our implantologist selects the ideal Nobel Biocare implant model (NobelActive, NobelParallel, or NobelReplace) based on your bone quality and treatment goals.

2

Precision Placement

The Nobel Biocare implant is placed using guided surgery protocols. The TiUnite surface technology promotes rapid osseointegration, allowing for faster healing and earlier loading.

3

Healing & Integration

During the osseointegration period (typically 8-12 weeks with Nobel Biocare's advanced surfaces), a temporary restoration maintains your aesthetics and function.

4

Final Restoration

A precision-milled Nobel Biocare abutment and custom porcelain or zirconia crown are placed, delivering a restoration engineered for lifelong performance and natural beauty.
