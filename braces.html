Traditional & Ceramic Braces

Dental braces remain the most versatile and effective orthodontic solution for correcting crowding, spacing, overbites, underbites, crossbites, and complex malocclusions. At Dr. Kathuria's Dentistry, our specialist orthodontists offer both high-grade stainless steel metal braces and discreet tooth-coloured ceramic braces.

Metal braces use self-ligating technology for faster treatment and fewer adjustments, while ceramic braces use translucent brackets that blend with your natural enamel, making them far less noticeable. Both options deliver outstanding results for patients of all ages.

Your Braces Journey

1

Consultation & Records

Comprehensive examination with digital X-rays, photographs, and 3D scans to plan your treatment precisely.

2

Braces Placement

Brackets are carefully bonded to your teeth and connected with arch wires. The process takes about 60-90 minutes and is painless.

3

Monthly Adjustments

Regular visits every 4-6 weeks for wire adjustments that progressively move your teeth into their ideal positions.

4

Removal & Retention

Braces are removed to reveal your new smile. Fixed and removable retainers ensure your results last a lifetime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Braces placement is painless. You may experience mild soreness for 3-5 days after placement and after each adjustment, easily managed with over-the-counter pain relief. Modern self-ligating brackets and low-friction wires significantly reduce discomfort compared to older systems.

Both deliver excellent results. Metal braces are more affordable and slightly more durable, making them ideal for complex cases and younger patients. Ceramic braces are preferred by adults and image-conscious patients due to their discreet appearance. Our orthodontist will help you decide.

You can eat most foods with braces, but should avoid very hard, sticky, or crunchy items that could damage the brackets (e.g., hard candy, caramel, popcorn kernels). We provide a detailed dietary guide at your placement appointment to help you adjust comfortably.
